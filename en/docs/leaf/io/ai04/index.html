AI04 LCD

Features

This leaf is equipped with a character LCD unit and two switches. The LCD unit is connected to the MCU leaf via I2C. The switch is connected to the digital input pin of the MCU and the P1 pin of the I2C Expander. To achieve low power consumption, the I2C Expander can be controlled to turn off the power supply with a load switch.
